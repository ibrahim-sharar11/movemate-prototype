## ‚úÖ BEST OPTION: OBS Studio (Free)

**Why:** Easy picture-in-picture, free, professional look

### Quick Setup:

1. **Download OBS Studio**
   - Go to: https://obsproject.com/
   - Download for Mac
   - Install (drag to Applications)

2. **Setup OBS:**
   
   **Add Screen Capture:**
   - Click "+" under "Sources"
   - Select "Display Capture"
   - Choose your display
   - Click "OK"
   
   **Add Webcam:**
   - Click "+" under "Sources"
   - Select "Video Capture Device"
   - Choose your camera (usually "FaceTime HD Camera")
   - Click "OK"
   
   **Position Your Face:**
   - Click on the webcam source
   - Drag it to bottom-right corner (or wherever you want)
   - Resize it (smaller, like a corner webcam)
   - Right-click ‚Üí "Filters" ‚Üí "Crop/Pad" to crop edges if needed

3. **Start Recording:**
   - Click "Start Recording" button
   - Do your demo
   - Click "Stop Recording"

4. **Find Your Video:**
   - File ‚Üí Show Recordings
   - Or check: Movies folder

## Alternative: QuickTime + Separate Webcam Recording

**Method:** Record screen with QuickTime, record face separately, combine later

1. **Record Screen:**
   - QuickTime ‚Üí New Screen Recording
   - Record your demo

2. **Record Face:**
   - QuickTime ‚Üí New Movie Recording
   - Record yourself explaining (use script)

3. **Combine in iMovie:**
   - Import both videos
   - Put screen recording as main
   - Add face video as picture-in-picture overlay
   - Position in corner
   - Export

## Alternative: Loom (Easiest with Face)

**Why:** Built-in webcam support, very easy

### Steps:

1. **Install Loom:**
   - Go to loom.com
   - Install Chrome extension

2. **Record:**
   - Click Loom icon
   - Choose "Screen + Camera"
   - Select your browser window
   - Position face window (drag to corner)
   - Click record

3. **Done:**
   - Auto-uploads to Loom
   - Get shareable link
   - Download video if needed

## üí° Tips:

### Face Position:
- **Bottom-right corner** (most common)
- Or bottom-left
- Keep it small but visible
- Don't block important UI elements

### Lighting:
- Face a window or light source
- Don't have light behind you
- Make sure face is clearly visible

### Background:
- Clean background (wall or blur)
- Not distracting
- Professional look

### Framing:
- Center your face in the webcam window
- Head and shoulders visible
- Look at camera when possible

## üìê OBS Studio Setup Details:

### Scene Setup:
1. Create new scene: "Screen + Face"
2. Add Display Capture (your screen)
3. Add Video Capture Device (webcam)
4. Right-click webcam ‚Üí "Transform" ‚Üí "Fit to Screen" (or resize manually)
5. Drag webcam to bottom-right
6. Make it smaller (maybe 320x240 or 640x480)

### Settings:
- **Video:**
  - Base Canvas: 1920x1080
  - Output: 1920x1080
- **Output:**
  - Recording Format: MP4
  - Encoder: x264 or Apple VT H264
  - Bitrate: 2500-4000 Kbps (good quality)
