## Quick Tips

1. **Be Specific:** Instead of "worked on code", say "implemented form validation in app.js"
2. **Show Collaboration:** Mention group discussions and decisions
3. **Realistic:** Don't overstate individual contributions
4. **Complete:** Fill in all sections
